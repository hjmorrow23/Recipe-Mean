Peanut Butter Chocolate Chip Cookies

Category: Dessert

Prep Time: 15 min

Cook Time: 8 min

Ingredients

  • Sugar 6 tbsp
  • Brown Sugar 6 tbsp
  • Butter (softened) 2tbsp
  • Vanilla Extract 1 tsp
  • 1 Egg
  • Peanut Butter 1 cup
  • Baking Soda 1/2 tsp
  • Salt 1/2 tsp
  • Flour 3/4 cup
  • Chocolate Chips 3/4 cup

Instructions

  1. Preheat over to 375 degrees Fahrenheit.
  2. Cream sugar, brown sugar and butter in large mixing bowl
  3. Break egg into mixture and add vanilla. Mix well
  4. Add Peanut butter, baking soda and salt into mixture.
  5. Add flour and chocolate chips, mixing slowly to start then at regular speed.
  6. Use tablespoon to form dough into balls making about 12-15 to a pan. Press on top with a fork in an X shape.
  7. Bake for 7 to 8 minutes. Cookies should be soft consistency. Let cool for 5-10 minutes.
